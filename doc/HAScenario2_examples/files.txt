WuKongComponents.xml                XML definitions of the standard set of components.
                                    (examples for Threshold and Temperature Sensor)

GEN_VirtualThresholdSuper.java      The generated part of a virtual wuclass, containing the constant definitions.
                                    (generated from WuKongComponents.xml)

VirtualThreshold.java               The implementation part of a virtual wuclass.

HAScenario2_Flow.xml                WuKong FlowXML definition for the HAScenario2 application.
                                    (generated from the Niagara config.bof file)

HAScenario2_Application.java        Java application for the HAScenario2 application.
                                    (generated by the translator from HAScenario2_Flow.xml and WuKongComponents.xml)

HAScenario2_RequiredWuObjects.xml   List of required WuObject
                                    (generated by the translator from HAScenario2_Flow.xml and WuKongComponents.xml)

HAScenario2_Mapping.java            Mapping of components to nodes/port numbers
                                    (generated by the master from HAScenario2_RequiredWuObjects.xml and WuKongComponents.xml,
                                    after inspecting the available WuClasses/WuObjects in the network)
